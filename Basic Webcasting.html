  Characteristics of a good stream  


Description

Basic webcasting substantially extends the reach of your meeting or event: one location, one event, yet a much larger audience. A webcast means that no one ever has to miss your meeting or event again. Participants follow your webcast live via their PC, Mac, tablet or smartphone. Not only can they see and hear the speaker(s), but it is also possible to facilitate direct interaction.
